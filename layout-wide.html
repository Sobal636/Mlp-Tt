TRINITY TRUST

About

This ministry was named as “TRINITY TRUST” and was registered as a non profit organization. Now by the grace of God, many years after its inception, Trinity has been growing and has fifteen full time missionaries ministering in twelve different locations in Kerala. We are standing in agreement with you for a hundred fold harvest of what you are sowing into this ministry sacrificially. OUR MISSION STATEMENT - “AS THE LORD LIVES WE WILL CONTINUE PREACHING TEACHING AND DISCIPLING PEOPLE OF ALL NATIONS” The Mission outreach takes inspiration from the Bible verse, Mat 25:40, which teaches us “And the King will answer them, Truly , I say to you, as you did it to one of the least of these my children, you did it to me”. We have now fifteen full time missionaries doing the Lord’s work in twelve different locations in Kerala.
